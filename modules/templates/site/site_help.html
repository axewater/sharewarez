Help / FAQ

Welcome to SharewareZ! This comprehensive guide will help you navigate and make the most of our platform's features.

Getting Started

  • Navigate through our extensive game library using the sidebar menu
  • Use the search bar at the top to quickly find specific games
  • Apply filters to narrow down your search by genre, platform, or release date
  • Click on any game to view detailed information, screenshots, and download options

Downloads

  • Click the download button on any game page to start the download process
  • Downloads are queued and processed automatically by our server
  • Track your download progress in the Downloads section
  • Downloaded files are available for 24 hours before being automatically removed

Your Library

  • Add games to your favorites by clicking the heart icon
  • Access your favorite games quickly from the sidebar
  • View your download history in the Downloads section
  • Customize your experience through the user preferences panel

Need Help?

If you need additional assistance or encounter any issues:

  • Contact an administrator through the support system
  • Check our FAQ section for common questions
  • Report any technical issues you encounter
